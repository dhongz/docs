### How to Import Google Search Console Data
<Steps>
<Step title="Click the `+` button on your Grid page"/>
<Step title="Select Google Search Console from the dropdown menu of import options"/>
<Step title="Connect to your Google Search Console account:">
   - Select an existing Google Search Console authentication from the dropdown
   - If you do not have an existing Google Search Console authentication, you'll need to authenticate with your Google account
   - Click **Continue**
</Step>
<Step title="Configure your data import:">
   - **Data Type**: Choose between:
     - **Queries**: Shows data for search terms that led to your site
     - **Pages**: Shows performance data for individual URLs
   - **Select Period**: Choose the time frame for your data:
     - Last 7 days
     - Last 28 days
     - Last 3 months
     - Last 6 months
     - Last 12 months
     - Last 16 months
   - Click **Continue**
</Step>
<Step title="Review and import the data to your Grid"/>   
</Steps>
Once imported, your Google Search Console data will be available in your Grid, ready to be analyzed and optimized with AirOps workflows. You can identify underperforming content, discover keyword opportunities, and create AI-powered optimizations to improve your search rankings.
