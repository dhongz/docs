## How to Configure the YouTube Integration Step

### Action Type

**Retrieve Video Captions**: Extract captions/transcript from your YouTube video

### Video URL or ID

Enter the YouTube video ID from your channel:

- Video ID: `dQw4w9WgXcQ`

### Key Output Fields

- `full_text`: Complete captions as a single string
- `transcript`: Array of timed caption segments
- `video_title`: Title of your video
- `video_id`: YouTube video identifier

## Common Use Cases

- Transform video content into blog posts
- Generate video summaries and key points
- Create show notes for podcast episodes
- Extract quotes for social media content
- Build searchable content from your video library

<Info>
**Note:** 

You must authenticate your YouTube account in AirOps Settings > Integrations before using this step.
</Info>
