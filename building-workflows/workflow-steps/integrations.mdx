CMS Integrations enable direct connections between your AirOps workflows and popular content management systems. These integrations allow you to publish, update, and retrieve content without manual copying and pasting, creating end-to-end automation for your content operations.

The CMS Integrations include:

<Columns cols={2}>
<Card title="Webflow" icon="webflow" href="/building-workflows/workflow-steps/integrations/webflow">
Create, update, and manage content on Webflow sites
</Card>
<Card title="WordPress" icon="wordpress" href="/building-workflows/workflow-steps/integrations/wordpress">
Publish and update posts and pages on WordPress sites
</Card>
<Card title="Shopify" icon="shopify" href="/building-workflows/workflow-steps/integrations/shopify">
Manage product descriptions and content on Shopify stores
</Card>
<Card title="Contentful" icon="database" href="/building-workflows/workflow-steps/integrations/contentful">
Create and update entries in Contentful spaces
</Card>
<Card title="Sanity" icon="database" href="/building-workflows/workflow-steps/integrations/sanity">
Fetch, create and update documents in Sanity projects
</Card>
<Card title="Strapi" icon="database" href="/building-workflows/workflow-steps/integrations/strapi">
Integrate with Strapi's headless CMS platform
</Card>
</Columns>

CMS Integrations transform how you manage content across your digital ecosystem by bridging the gap between AI-powered content generation and your publishing platforms. These connections allow you to automate the entire content lifecycleâ€”from ideation and creation to publication and updates.

With CMS Integrations, you can build workflows that automatically push optimized content to your website, update product descriptions across your e-commerce store, maintain consistent brand messaging across multiple platforms, and implement large-scale content strategy changes without manual intervention.

<Info>
**Note:**

CMS Integrations are designed for Workflow applications. Authentication requires appropriate admin permissions for each respective platform.
</Info>
